Electronic interaction

Total Cost: £225

To add electronic integration into the exhibit, three seperate circuits are required to do this. These allow for four custom gravitational wave signsals to be injected into the interferometer, for the types of signals LIGO, VIRGO and Kagra are likely to detect. Breakdowns for each small electronic circuit are provided separately.
